## Cross-Cutting Elements

### All Articles Should Include:

**Visual Elements:**
- 1-2 diagrams or frameworks (the Sovereignty Stack, etc.)
- Pull quotes for Twitter sharing
- Section headers that work as standalone insights

**Voice Markers:**
- First-person experience ("I failed," "I built")
- Contrarian framing ("Everyone says X, but...")
- Actionable specifics (not theory—"Here's how")
- Transparent vulnerability (mistakes build trust)

**Engagement Mechanics:**
- Questions embedded in text
- Scorecards or self-assessments
- "Tweet this" pull quotes
- Comment prompts at the end

**Conversion Elements:**
- Lead magnet offer (checklist, scorecard, template)
- Email subscription CTA
- Community invitation
- Paid tier tease (deeper content)
