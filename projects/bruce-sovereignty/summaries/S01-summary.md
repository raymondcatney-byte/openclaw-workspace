## Key Decisions Made

- **Scoring threshold:** 19/25 (76%) = minimum sovereignty
- **Legacy target lower:** 3/5 acceptable (long-term pillar, builds over time)
- **Audit frequency:** Daily quick-check + weekly deep audit
- **Time budget:** <10 minutes for full weekly audit
