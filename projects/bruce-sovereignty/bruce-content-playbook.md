## Thread Structures

### Type A: The Manifesto Thread (Balaji-style)
**Structure:**
1. Hook: Contradiction or prediction
2. Setup: Here's what's wrong with current narrative
3. Thesis: Here's the alternative reality I'm building toward
4. Pillars: 3-5 supporting arguments
5. Call: Join the movement/build the thing

**Example Opening:**
"By 2030, there will be two classes of people:

Those who operate through 5+ personas, with capital diversified across 8+ asset classes, with information networks that see moves before they happen.

And those who wonder why they keep getting front-run.

Sovereignty isn't a luxury. It's becoming survival.

Here's the infrastructure you need to build:"

### Type B: The Educational Thread (Sahil-style)
**Structure:**
1. Hook: Relatable problem
2. Promise: Here's the framework
3. Body: 5-7 clear steps with examples
4. Recap: Summary visual or list
5. CTA: Follow for more / read the deep dive

**Example Opening:**
"I spent 6 years learning to be invisible online.

Not 'privacy conscious.' Actually invisible.

I made every mistake. Lost money. Burned personas. Revealed things I shouldn't have.

Here's the 6-layer identity separation system I use now:

[thread continues]"

### Type C: The Contrarian Thread (pmarca-style)
**Structure:**
1. Hot take: The thing everyone believes is wrong
2. Evidence: Here's what I see that you don't
3. Implications: If I'm right, here's what happens
4. Positioning: This is why I'm building X

**Example Opening:**
"The 'renewable energy transition' is just swapping oil colonialism for lithium colonialism.

70% of critical minerals in the Global South.

Profits captured by the Global North.

78 million tonnes of solar waste by 2050 that nobody's planning for.

The greenest energy is the energy you don't need because you built efficient systems.

But that doesn't sell batteries."

## Timing & Velocity Strategy

### The Naval Rhythm (Quality)
- 1 tweet per week
- Make it timeless
- Let it compound
- Focus on quotability

### The Balaji Rhythm (Narrative)
- 1 manifesto thread per month
- 3-5 supporting tweets per week
- Build toward a coherent worldview
- Create memetic phrases that spread

### The Sahil Rhythm (Education)
- 1 educational thread per week
- Daily single-tweet insights
- Repackage Substack content
- Consistency over virality

**Hybrid: The Sovereign Pulse**

- **Monday:** Educational thread (Sahil-style) — practical sovereignty mechanics

## Voice Checklist (Before Posting)

- [ ] Is this something Bruce Wayne would actually say?
- [ ] Is there a specific insight, not just opinion?
- [ ] Would this still make sense in 5 years?
- [ ] Does it serve the sovereignty mission?
- [ ] Is there a surprise or unexpected angle?
- [ ] Can someone quote this in a meeting to sound smart?

**If 4+ are yes:** Post it.
**If 3 or fewer:** Rewrite or delete.
