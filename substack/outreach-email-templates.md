# Outreach Email Template: First 50 Prospects

## The Strategy

**Target:** Family offices, wealth managers, strategists with $50M+ AUM
**Method:** Personal, warm introduction, not cold pitch
**Goal:** 10 subscriptions (free) from 50 emails = 20% conversion

---

## Template A: Direct Value Proposition

**Subject:** Navigating the tension

**Body:**

```
[First Name],

I'm writing to a small group of people who manage significant capital 
while sensing the ground shifting beneath them.

You may have noticed: the old playbooks aren't working as well. 
The frameworks that built the wealth don't clearly map to what's 
coming next.

I write a weekly briefing on exactly this—the operational real beneath 
the simulation. Not predictions. Constraints. The forces that determine 
which futures are possible, regardless of which futures we prefer.

Recent issues covered:
• Resource sovereignty (energy, minerals, biotech)
• Monetary architecture (CBDCs, financial repression)
• Strategic realignment (who controls what, and why it matters)

The format is concise. One email. Sunday morning. No noise.

If you're interested: [Substack link]

If not, no worries—this is the only email you'll receive from me.

Best,
[Name]
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 0:** Initial email (one of above)

**Day 7:** If no response
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

[First Name],

Quick follow-up. No pressure if the timing isn't right.

If you're interested in the briefing, here's the link: [link]

If not, all good—I won't follow up again.

Best,
[Name]
```

**Day 30:** If subscribed but not engaged
```
Subject: Is this hitting the mark?

[First Name],

You've been getting the dispatch for a few weeks. Curious: is it 
useful? Missing the mark? Too much noise?

I'm refining based on feedback. Your input would help.

Reply if you have 30 seconds.

Best,
[Name]
```

## Target List Building

**Sources:**
1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator
   - Title: "Family Office" OR "CIO" OR "Managing Director"
   - Company size: 11-50 (typical family office)
   - Location: Major financial centers (NYC, London, Singapore, Zurich)

2. Conference attendee lists (if accessible)
   - Milken Institute
   - SALT
   - Family Office Exchange

3. Mutual connections
   - Ask existing network for intros to 2-3 people each

4. Twitter/X
   - Follow people who engage with similar thinkers
   - Check who subscribes to comparable publications

**Tracking:**
Create spreadsheet with:
- Name
- Firm
- Source
- Template used
- Date sent
- Response
- Status (pending/subscribed/declined)

## Conversion Optimization

**Subject line A/B test:**
- A: "Navigating the tension"
- B: "Briefing on [specific topic they're interested in]"
- C: "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out"

**Best performing:** C (referral-based), then B (specific), then A (intriguing)

**Send time:**
- Tuesday 9 AM EST (best for B2B)
- Avoid Friday (deleted in Monday cleanup)
- Never Monday morning (inbox flooded)

**Personalization elements:**
- Always use first name
- Reference specific topic if known
- Mention mutual connection if exists
- One specific detail shows it's not mass email

## The First 50 Strategy

**Week 1:** Identify 50 targets, send 10 emails (test templates)
**Week 2:** Analyze response rates, refine, send 20 more
**Week 3:** Send remaining 20
**Week 4:** Follow-up non-responders

**Goal:** 10 subscribers from 50 emails (20% conversion)

If conversion <10%, revise template or targeting.
If conversion >30%, scale to next 100 immediately.
