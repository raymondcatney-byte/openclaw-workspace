Best,

## Preparation for Appearance

### Pre-Interview

1. **Listen to 3 recent episodes**
   - Note host's style (interrupts? lets guests ramble?)
   - Note typical episode length
   - Identify 2-3 previous guests with similar expertise

2. **Prepare 3 key stories**
   - Personal journey (2 minutes max)
   - Case study/example (concrete, specific)
   - Contrarian take (memorable, defensible)

3. **Prepare 5 quotable lines**
   - Aphorisms that can be clipped for social media
   - These become the marketing hook for the episode

4. **Have 3 specific predictions**
   - Dated ("within 6 months...")
   - Measurable ("X will happen to Y")
   - Confident (not hedged with "maybe" or "possibly")

### During Interview

**Do:**
- Answer the question asked (not the question you want to answer)
- Use concrete examples (names, dates, numbers)
- Pause before answering (seems thoughtful, not scripted)
- Reference specific thinkers/designers ("As [Name] would say...")

**Don't:**
- Pitch your Substack (host will do this if they like you)
- Use jargon without explaining
- Go longer than 2 minutes on any single point
- Disagree with the host unless it's substantive

### Post-Interview

1. **Thank host via email** (same day)
2. **Share episode when live** (Twitter, Substack, email list)
3. **Clip best moments** (if host doesn't provide clips)
4. **Follow up 3 months later** (with new angle/pitch)

## Conversion Tracking

Each appearance should generate:
- 100-500 free subscribers (Tier 1 podcast)
- 50-200 free subscribers (Tier 2 podcast)
- 5-20 paid conversions (within 30 days)

**If below these numbers:**
- Review: Did you give away too much value in the free interview?
- Review: Was your call-to-action clear?
- Review: Did the episode title/description include your name/concept?

## The 10-Podcast Strategy

**Month 1-2:**
- 2 Tier 2 podcasts (test material, build confidence)
- Track conversion rates

**Month 3-4:**
- 3 Tier 2 podcasts (refined pitch)
- 1 Tier 1 podcast (if warm intro available)

**Month 5-6:**
- 2 Tier 1 podcasts (goal: All-In or Pomp)
- 2 Tier 2 podcasts

**Goal:** 10 appearances in 6 months → 1,000+ subscribers
