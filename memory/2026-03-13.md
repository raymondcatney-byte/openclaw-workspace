### Key Decision: Pivot to ElizaOS Swarm Architecture

**Original Approach (Rejected):**
- 8 custom personas based on real investors (Buffett, Burry, Wood, etc.)
- Felt too generic, too much about investment philosophies rather than actionable agents
- User requested tighter integration with proven frameworks

**New Approach (Adopted):**
- ElizaOS "Marc AIndreessen" style swarm of coordinated agents
- 5 specialized agents with clear roles, not 8 deep personas
- TypeScript-native, crypto-focused, production-proven
